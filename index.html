our process

Our Process

We follow a detailed step by step web development process to make it as simple and clear as possible. Our process makes it efficient & productive for both us and our clients.
01

Project Definition

Layout the requirements and goals of the project. Set expectations in terms of the timelines and delivery. Note details that would be needed from the clients. What problem are we trying to solve and how our agency would try and solve the same.

Wireframes and Site Architecture

Before building the site with code we give our clients the layouts and wireframes for them to understand the feel and functionality of the website and get necessary feedback to make sure we are in tune with the expectations of our clients.
02
03

Visual Design

Once clients are happy with the wireframes and layouts provided in the previous step we visually design the site and provide these to the client to get the necessary feedback and make changes to the design if required.

Site Development

Once designed are approved by the client we move to one of the most important steps in the process, to put the design into code and make the code efficient so that it runs fast. We make sure the site looks good on both desktops and mobile phones.
04
05

Site Testing

Once coding for the site is completed. We test the site thoroughly and give an opportunity to our clients to do the same. We make sure the site functions as required on both desktops and mobile phones and remove any bugs found in our code. This is an opportunity for our clients to remove any functionality from the site they don't need.

Launch

We get the required domain and server details from the client or acquire the same if the client doesn't have them. Then we launch the site on the server under the given domain name. At this stage we do a test again of the site to make sure it functions as required.
06
07

Site Maintenance

We provide a free period of site maintenance service and give a certain number of changes that a client would want to make on the site after the launch. We also handover all the required information to the client if they want to maintain the site themselves so that they are not dependent on us to do the same. We charge a nominal hourly rate for any changes if we do not maintain the site.
